What to expect at a color correction consultation

A transparent look at strand tests, realistic timelines, and why correction work starts with a plan.

May 29, 2026 · Tyra Hair Studio

A correction consultation begins with history: previous color, at-home products, heat habits, and the look you want next.

The safest plan is usually staged. That protects the hair and gives each appointment a clear target instead of chasing a result in one risky sitting.

Photos help, but daylight photos of your current hair help even more. They give your stylist a better read on tone, banding, and condition.
