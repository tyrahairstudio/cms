Welcome to

Tyra Hair Studio

Dimensional color, gloss, and cuts shaped around real movement.

Welcome to Tyra Hair Studio, a warm Cypress salon where healthy hair meets effortless elegance. Every appointment is guided by your hair history, your daily routine, and the look you want to feel confident wearing beyond the chair.

From soft blonding and lived-in brunettes to gloss, tone, and detailed cuts, we create polished color with natural movement, lasting shine, and a finish that still feels like you.
